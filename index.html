About us

Some words about us

Cyknight is the computer club at Delhi Public School, Indirapuram. It is a 17 member organisation with its members standing out in various fields like computer programming, computer quizzing, web design & development, digital imaging, 3d design & animation, 2d animation,video and audio editing, photography etc.

  • Mission - We deliver uniqueness and quality
  • Skills - Delivering fast and excellent results
